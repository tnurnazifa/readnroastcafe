Our History

Homely Book Cafe

Read & Roast Café began its journey in October
2018, founded by Nur Aizattie Binti Awang and a
team of passionate young individuals from Pahang.
Combining a love for books and coffee, the café was
envisioned as a cozy space for readers, thinkers,
and coffee enthusiasts to connect and unwind.



Starting with a single location, Read & Roast
quickly gained popularity for its welcoming
atmosphere, curated book collection, and
artisanal coffee. By 2022, the café had expanded to
10 branches across Peninsular Malaysia, with an
ambitious goal of opening 3 branches nationwide by
the end of the year.



Read & Roast Café continues to thrive as a hub
for knowledge, creativity, and community, bringing
people together one cup and one story at a time.
