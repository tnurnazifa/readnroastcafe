About Us

The No. 1 Choice Coffee Shop In Malaysia

Our café is proudly run by a team of young talents from Pahang, led by Nur Aizattie Binti Awang as our Chief Executive Officer. Established in October 2018, we've grown steadily with a vision to bring people together over great coffee and books.

For 2024, our goal is to expand to 10 branches nationwide. Currently, we're thrilled to serve our community across 3 branches in Peninsular Malaysia.
